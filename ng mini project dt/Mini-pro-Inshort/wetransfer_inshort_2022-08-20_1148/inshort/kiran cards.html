Tata Motors to buy Ford's manufacturing plant in gugrat for 726 crore
short by Pragya Swastik / 06:45 pm on 16 Mar 2022,Wednesday

Tata Motors on Sunday announced that it has signed an agreement to buy Ford Motor's manufacturing plant in Sanand, Gujarat for ₹726 crore. The agreement between the two companies includes land, assets and transfer of all eligible employees. The development comes nearly a year after Ford Motor said it will cease local manufacturing in India due to operating losses.

Factory line is upgrades distrupt Tesla's china-made car sales in july
short by Ria Kapoor / 05:10 pm on on 13 Dec 2021,Monday

Tesla sold 28,217 Chinese-made vehicles in July as against 78,906 vehicles in June as production was disrupted by a scheduled upgrade to its factory lines in Shanghai. The upgrade aims to raise the factory's weekly output to around 22,000 units compared to around 17,000 units in June. Tesla exported 19,756 Model 3s and Model Ys from China last month.

Bajaj Auto two-wheeler sales drop by 5% in July to 3.15 lakh units
short by Hiral Goyal / 06:45 pm on 16 Mar 2022,Wednesday

Bajaj Auto on Monday reported a 5% fall in its total two-wheeler sales at 3,15,054 units in July 2022 as against 3,30,569 units in the corresponding period last year.
