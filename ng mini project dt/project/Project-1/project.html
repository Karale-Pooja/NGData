India under leadership of PM Modi has given us a
breath of life: Sri Lanka President
short by Arshiya Chopra / 01:34 pm on 03 Aug 2022,Wednesday

Expressing gratitude towards PM Narendra Modi and people of India, newly elected Sri Lankan President Ranil Wickremesinghe on Wednesday said, "Government of India under the leadership of PM Modi has given us a breath of life." "I wish to specially mention the assistance provided by India, our closest neighbour, in our efforts for economic revitalisation, " Wickremesinghe added.
