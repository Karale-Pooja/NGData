
Angular Directives:
	
	DOM: Document Object Model and it consist of structure of html elements as well as javascript events.
	
	Directives are responsible for adding and removing elements from the DOM as well as chnaging the behaviour of the DOM.
	
	There are three types of directives.
	1. Component directives.
	2. Structural Directives.
	3. Attribute Directives.
	
		1. Structural Directives:
			Structural Directives are responsible for adding and removing elements from the DOM.
			*ngIf, *ngFor, *ngSwitch
			
		2. Attribute Directives:
			Attribute directives are responsible for chnaging the behaviour of the DOM.
			ngStyle and ngClass
